    Heuristic Analasis of Star Wars Empire At War

    Visibility of system status: In Star Wars Empire At War when a ship loses a Hardpoint(Weapon or utility), a sounds is played which informs you of this. For example when a ship loses their engine you will be informed that that ship has lost its engine. Look at 2:28 When a capital ship of yours or the enemy appears on the map, it will be indicated by a large red dot on the minimap. As also can be seen here are the bright red dots, these indicate areas that your units are under fire. This can get confusing and lead to micro-management, although from the survey this method tends to be prefered in RTS.
